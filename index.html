Get To Know Us

Our vision is to grow our multi-disciplinary team in order to offer a broad spectrum of specialist Engineering supplies and Electrical services to become our Clients' preferred Supplier through excellence and efficiency in all aspects of the project life cycle. Whether it is a PLC Supply or complete Automation project, implementation of a project management system, or programme management of a multi-facetted initiative, Jhing Industrial is a leading practice that will exceed expectations and set new standards!

Our Mission is to offer excellent service in each of our professional disciplines, in accordance with statutory practices, codes of conduct and integrity, thereby developing our team and providing a leading platform from which to service the in particular, our valued Clients.

With a commitment to efficiency and reliability, we supply top-tier products and offer expert support to keep your operations running smoothly. Our nationwide shipping ensures fast, convenient delivery, making us your go-partner for all automation and industrial supply needs.

  • PLC & Automation Solutions – Supply and integration of programmable logic controllers (PLCs), HMIs, and full automation systems.
  • High-performance sensors, valves, actuators, pneumatic/hydraulic components and etc.
  • Nationwide Shipping – Fast, reliable delivery of industrial supplies across the country, ensuring minimal downtime.

Our experienced and focused teams are well placed and equipped and contain the correct experience and qualifications to carry out their functions with diligence and skill. It is our vision that Jhing Industrial may be of service to all prospective Clients in the Private, Professional and Public sector.
